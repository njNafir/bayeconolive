Who

We are

MISSION

To establish and operate BayEZ as a world class facility for our valued entrepreneurs and stakeholders while serving as a pioneer Economic Zone (EZ) for the sustainable growth of Bangladesh.

VISION

To become the global ambassador for Bangladesh and a catalyst for the economic and social development of our country, thereby transforming Bangladesh into a middle income country by 2021 and a developed country by 2041 through the establishment and continuation of world class customer services at our art facilities..

CORE VALUES

• Nurturing and serving the needs of all people (employees, customers, and those in our community) while providing them with the opportunities to reach their full potential. • Structuring, implementing and applying world-class policies for the development and operation of our economic zone for the benefit of all our stakeholders.
